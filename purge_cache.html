Cache purge bookmarklet

It might just do the trick

This bookmarklet helps you clear Cloudflare's cache on a recently updated post. It probably won't help for posts that show a 404.

Instructions

First, mouse-down on the button link below (but don't complete the click) and drag it up to your toolbar:

Cache purge bookmarklet

It will create a bookmarklet called "Cache purge bookmarklet".

When you run into a page that doesn't show a recent update, click the bookmarklet and paste the page's URL into the little pop-up box.

If Cloudflare successfully purges the cache you'll see a pop-up saying so. If it doesn't successfully purge the cache you will see a pop-up saying it failed.

If you click the little check-box that disallows pop-ups from cpr.org then you are out of luck.
